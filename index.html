Important Notes

Easy OTP is only for quick browser use and temporary TOTP access.

Saved keys stay only in your browser using local storage. They may disappear if you clear browser cache, cookies, local storage, browser data, or use a cleaner tool.

For important accounts, it is safer to use dedicated authenticator apps such as Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, Authy, or similar apps.
